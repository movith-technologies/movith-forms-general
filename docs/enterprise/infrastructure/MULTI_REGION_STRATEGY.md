# MULTI REGION STRATEGY

## 1. OBJECTIVE

Support geographic expansion and data residency requirements.

---

## 2. STAGE APPROACH

Stage 1:
- Single region

Stage 2:
- Multi-region read replicas

Stage 3:
- Active-active deployment

---

## 3. DATA RESIDENCY

Enterprise tenants may request:

- Region-specific data storage
- EU-only or TR-only hosting

---

## 4. ROUTING STRATEGY

Use:

- Global load balancer
- Geo-based routing

---

## 5. FAILOVER STRATEGY

Automatic failover:

- Health checks
- Traffic rerouting
- Database failover

---

## 6. AI PROVIDER CONSIDERATION

AI region selection must consider:

- Latency
- Data residency rules
